MPC Plastering & General Building

Based in Huddersfield, MPC Plastering & General Building provides professional building and finishing work across West Yorkshire. From single-room repairs to full property upgrades, we keep it simple: tidy work, reliable timelines, and a finish you can trust.

We cover plastering, rendering (including silicone render), conversions, refurbishments, bathrooms, kitchens, paving, driveways and structural alterations. If you’re unsure what’s needed, we’ll advise and quote clearly.
